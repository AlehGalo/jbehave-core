JBehave dependencies are defined in the jbehave-distribution POM.

Choose the desired version from:

http://mvnrepository.com/artifact/org.jbehave/jbehave-distribution/

If you use Maven or Ivy or Grape, you can dependencies declaratively from the POM.

If you use Ant, download the POM with the desired version, rename it to pom.xml,
and execute Ant target in build.xml provided:

$ ant copy-dependencies
